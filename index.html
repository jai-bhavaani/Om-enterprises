Company Timeline

Our 20+ Year Journey to Excellence

2004 - The Beginning

OM Enterprises was founded by Mr. Ashok Pal on Jan 25th, 2004, with a vision to provide reliable excavation and civil contracting services in Mumbai.

2010 - Building Reputation

Successfully completed foundational projects for major clients like B.E. Billimoria & Co. Ltd., building a reputation for reliability and quality.

2015 - Scaling Operations

Expanded the fleet with modern piling rigs and heavy machinery, taking on complex, large-scale projects like the Welspun Group site in Thane.

2020 - Landmark Projects

Became a trusted partner for top-tier developers, executing major projects for HCC (Hindustan Construction Company) and Prestige Estates Projects Ltd.

2024+ (Today) - Industry Leader

Today, our 20+ years of experience are fully engaged, managing major 'In Progress' sites for industry titans like L&T Construction and Raymond Ltd.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What types of excavation services do you provide? â–¼

We provide comprehensive excavation services including site preparation, foundation digging, land clearing, grading, trenching, backfilling, and demolition work. Our services cover residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across India.

How do you ensure safety on construction sites? â–¼

Safety is our top priority. We follow strict safety protocols, conduct regular equipment maintenance, provide comprehensive training to our operators, use modern safety equipment, and comply with all Indian construction safety standards and regulations.

What areas do you serve? â–¼

We primarily serve Mumbai, Thane, and surrounding areas in Maharashtra. We also undertake large-scale projects across India including Delhi, Pune, Bangalore, and other major cities depending on project requirements.

How long does a typical excavation project take? â–¼

Project duration varies based on size, complexity, and site conditions. A residential foundation typically takes 3-7 days, while larger commercial projects may take several weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process.

Do you provide equipment rental services? â–¼

Yes, we offer equipment rental services with skilled operators. Our fleet includes excavators (EX-200, EX-210, EX-380), JCB loaders, piling rigs, and DTH drilling machines. Contact us for rental rates and availability.
